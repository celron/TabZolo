How it works?

TabZolo aims to be as simple to use as possible. Once installed, just click the TabZolo icon and it will hide all your inactive tabs and windows leaving you with your current tab to work with. TabZolo will prevent you from opening other tabs or windows while enabled.

What if I want my tabs back?

Simply disable TabZolo by clicking the icon and all your original tabs and windows will be restored back.

Why would you make such EVIL extension?

David's main motivation was to gain focus on one thing only and felt it would be nice to mess around with Chrome extensions to solve that pet peeve. Leo was intrigued with the project and decided to jump to the project to improve his skills.
